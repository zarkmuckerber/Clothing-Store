About Us

Meet Our CEO

With just a sewing machine, fabric, and a vision, Muhammad Sumbul transformed his humble home into the epicenter of creative innovation. November 9th, 2012, marked the beginning of a fashion odyssey. Armed with determination, he set out to redefine the clothing experience in the DN, Malaysia. Each stitch and design choice was driven by a deep commitment to bring better clothing to millions, ensuring that every wardrobe had the touch of elegance and sophistication.

Eco Friendly Fashion

We are committed to redefining the fashion industry by embracing eco-friendly practices. Our garments are crafted exclusively from sustainable materials, thoughtfully sourced to minimize environmental impact. From organic cotton that caresses the skin to recycled fabrics that breathe new life into fashion, each piece tells a story of conscientious creation.
